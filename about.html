Early Career Foundation

Ryan's journey began as an inside sales representative, selling cutting-edge technology training to CIOs, CTOs, and VPs at the largest Investment Banks and Brokerages worldwide. His exceptional performance earned him the #1 national ranking, leading to rapid promotions to Manager and then Senior Manager within just 2.5 years.

Breaking New Ground

After his initial success, Ryan transitioned to a complex sales position at a privately held managed services provider with zero customer base in the Chicago market. Through relentless determination and strategic execution, he transformed this challenge into opportunity, becoming a multi-year President's Club winner and earning equity in the company.

Leadership Excellence

This foundation launched Ryan into a transformative leadership role where he was responsible for turning around a struggling business unit. He not only made it the top-performing office but also created an enterprise team from scratch and developed a sustainable recurring revenue engine.

The Whale Boss Era

Over the last decade, Ryan has achieved the remarkable feat of growing a division from 0 to $30M ARR with only 4 salespeople—no marketing team, no lead generation army. This extraordinary efficiency became the foundation for Whale Boss, his consultancy that helps other organizations replicate these systematic results.

Teaching and Impact

Today, Ryan has taught over 800 CROs, VPs, and Leaders his proprietary Enterprise Sales frameworks. His client roster includes industry giants like Google, Amazon Web Services, Stripe, Salesforce, and Uber. He's recognized as a leading voice in how AI will impact sales today and in the future.

Philosophy

Ryan's mission is to cut decades off sales learning curves, enabling professionals to rapidly scale their results while creating time freedom and designing their ideal lifestyle. His approach focuses on systematic, repeatable processes rather than traditional sales tactics.
